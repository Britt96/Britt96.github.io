Body Mass Index (BMI) is an indicator of body fatness based on one's height and weight. It can be used to screen people for obesity, which increases their risk of diabetes and heart disease.
BMI Weight
< 18.5 Underweight
18.5 to 24.9 Healthy Weight
24.9 to 29.9 Overweight
30 or higher Obese
